FAQ

How much does it cost to build a house?

The cost of building a house depends on various factors such as location, size, materials used, and design complexity. On average, the cost ranges from $150 to $300 per square foot. We provide a detailed estimate after the initial consultation and design phase.

Can I make changes to the design during construction?

While it’s possible to make changes during construction, it can lead to delays and additional costs. We recommend finalizing the design before construction begins to avoid any issues. However, our team will work with you to accommodate changes as much as possible.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we offer warranties on both the materials used and the workmanship. The specifics of the warranty will be provided during the contract phase, ensuring your peace of mind.

Do you handle all the necessary permits and inspections?

Yes, we take care of all the necessary permits and inspections required for the construction of your home. We ensure that everything complies with local building codes and regulations.

Do you offer custom home designs?

Yes, we specialize in custom home designs tailored to meet your specific needs and preferences. Our team of architects and designers will work closely with you to create a unique design that reflects your vision.
